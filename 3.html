It began with a swim in the ocean.

Suddenly I realized that she had gone much farther away from shore than she expected and, to her great dismay, there was a pair of killer whales swimming toward her.

The whales circled around her several times while she did her best not to panic.

After several minutes of playful dives and underwater acrobatics, the whales swam away just as suddenly as they had appeared.

What happened next was terrifying. The ocean swelled up, curled over, and slammed me back onto the beach.
